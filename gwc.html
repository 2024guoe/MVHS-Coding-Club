What is GWC?


Girls Who Code is a non-profit organization that aims to close the gender gap in the tech field. Although their programs are targeted towards girls, women, or nonbinary individuals, they welcome any member who wishes to join their clubs irrespective of gender or gender identity. The GWC Clubs program includes a multitude of activities, videos, and projects to learn coding for kids from 3rd-12th grade. Aside from their extensive Girls Who Code Clubs program, they also have Summer Programs and an alumini network with college and career programs. Girls Who Code has more than 8,500 programs worldwide and sponsered by large companies such as Apple, Logitech, and JPMorgan.


Learn more about Girls Who Code here: https://girlswhocode.com/about-us
